The Blinky Bot Exercise

In this excercise, we learned a lot about soldering and even had the oppurtunity for some hands on experience. We were instructed carefully on how to handle a soldering iron and the safety precautions we should take. Although certain parts were tricky, it was ultimately a success! After completing the blinky bot, we took the measurements. Although it says Amps on the picture it is miliamps, but Mr. Hallace told me it was amps which I believe he wasn't paying attention when he said it!

The most enjoyable moment of this unit was creating the blinky bots. Although somewhat basic, being able to make something usable (to a degree) brings about a sense of fulfillment (Question 2.4).
I realized about myself that I'm much better at soldering than I thought I was. I used to do soldering in the past but it seems that as I grew older, I became better despite the fact that I haven't done soldering in so long. I'm also surprised I didn't hurt myself (Question 3.2)!
There were many areas of risk as using a soldering iron introduces a lot of hazards if not used properly with proper safety precautions. There was a risk of me hurting myself, or even worse, introducing an electric hazard by accidentally burning the insulation on one of the wires (Question 4.2).
My involvment in this unit allowed me to appreciate the power of breadboards when it came to prototyping. Knowing their power, I will definitely use them for prototyping any PCB's in the future (Question 5.2).

Breadboard Activity 1 and 3

In breadboard activity one, the circuit is simple where a single LED is connected with a battery and a 330 ohm resistor. The 330 ohm resistor is necessary so as not to damage the LED. The resistance in this circuit decreases the flow of current from the battery and thus reduces the amount of energy the LED is recieving.

Breadboard Activity 2

In breadboard activity two, the circuit becomes slightly more complex as 2 LED's are now connected in parallel. This results in the current dividing between 2 branches. Parallel connections are useful for reliability and maximum voltage transfer to each individual branch. This is why we see that the LED's, despite being 2, light as brightly as one LED was. The only difference (which isn't visible) is faster depletion of the battery.

Breadboard Activity 4

The final breadboard activity combines both series and parallel connections. The first LED is connected in series whereas the other 2 are connected in parallel. A 330 ohm resistor is placed to limit the flow of current as to not damage the LED's. The first LED (the one connected in series) lights brighter than the other 2 LED's because it recieves more current. Since the first LED is connected in series, the current does not diverge whereas when it branches out, it splits into 2, resulting in less energy reaching the other 2 LED's.

Game Showcase Video

This game is similar to an alien invader game with the differeces being that Shelly, the turtle, is the spaceship and the invaders are the plastic waste. The player has 3 lives and must advance through 5 levels to win the game. They must not collide with the plastic or any of its projectiles along with disallowing them from crossing the bottom edge of the screen. This game aims to raise awareness about the importance of cleansing the ocean from plastic pollution to preserve marine life and habitats.

I responded to challenges I faced by researching the issue and identifying the problem. I would continue to try different things, read documentation and forums to understand where the problem was and fix it (Question 1.2).
Although I had some experience in game development, this unit further boradened my understanding on the process of building a game and made me consider pursuing it further in my free time (Question 3.3).
The most enjoyable moment was seeing my code work after all the time and effort that was spent into making it work. Seeing this kept me encouraged and motivated (Question 2.4).
I learned about myself that I love coding and that I enjoy game development more than I thought I would. I also learned that i enjoyed challenges and overcoming them even mmore so (Question 5.1).

Main Menu

When the game first starts, the screen is automatically at the main menu. The main menu includes two options under the title where the player can either: start playing the game or view / read instructions. They can return to the main menu by clicking escape, however, doing so will result in loss of progress if the game is aborted while playing.

Instructions

This option allows the player to view and read instructions. Upon clicking, they will be redirected to a page with guidlines on how to play the game, controls, objectives, etc. To escape this page the player can click the escape key and by doing so they are redirected to the main menu. This option is available at all times for the player's referance.
